# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard



---

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides comprehensive insights into car sales performance, inventory management, revenue analysis, and pricing trends across multiple automotive brands and models.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor KPIs, identify top-performing brands, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The business faced challenges in:

- Tracking sales performance across brands
- Monitoring inventory availability
- Analyzing revenue contribution
- Understanding pricing patterns
- Generating centralized reports

Manual reporting processes made decision-making slow and inefficient.

---

## ✅ Solution

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to:

- Centralize sales reporting
- Analyze revenue trends
- Track inventory distribution
- Compare brand performance
- Monitor vehicle pricing
- Evaluate engine-type revenue

## 🔍 Dashboard Insights

### 🚗 Top Revenue Brands
- Nissan, Tesla, and Mercedes generated the highest revenue.

### 📦 Inventory Analysis
- Some vehicle models maintain higher stock levels, indicating strong availability.

### ⚡ Engine Type Performance
- Diesel and Electric vehicles contribute the most revenue.

### 💎 Premium Vehicle Analysis
- High-priced models significantly contribute to total sales value.

### 📈 Sales Status Tracking
- Large available inventory highlights opportunities for improved sales conversion.
