Car Sales Dashboard
Overview

Interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze car sales, inventory, pricing trends, and revenue performance across multiple brands and years.

Business Problem

The business lacked a centralized reporting system to monitor:

Sales performance
Inventory distribution
Revenue contribution by brand
Vehicle pricing trends
Solution

Developed an interactive dashboard that provides:

Revenue analysis
Inventory tracking
Brand performance insights
Engine type analysis
Year-wise filtering
Key KPIs
Total Revenue: 292M
Average Car Price: 59K
Total Brands Analyzed: 500
Total Pricing Value: 30M
Key Business Insights
Nissan, Tesla, and Mercedes generate the highest revenue.
Diesel and Electric vehicles contribute the most revenue.
Large available inventory indicates opportunities for stronger sales conversion.
Premium vehicle models drive high-value sales.
