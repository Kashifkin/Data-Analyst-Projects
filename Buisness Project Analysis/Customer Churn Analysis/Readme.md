## Dashboard Overview

Developed an interactive Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard using Power BI to analyze customer retention, identify churn patterns, and generate actionable business insights. The dashboard helps businesses understand why customers leave and supports data-driven retention strategies.

## Dashboard Highlights
Churn Rate: 27%
Total Customers: 6,418
Total Churn Customers: 1,732
New Joiners: 411

## Key Business Insights
Customers with shorter tenure showed higher churn risk.
Female customers contributed a larger share of churn.
Customers using Mailed Check payment method had higher churn rates.
Customers aged 50+ were more likely to leave.
Certain states showed significantly higher churn concentration.

## Business Problems Solved
Identified major factors causing customer churn.
Improved visibility into customer behavior and retention trends.
Enabled targeted retention strategies using customer segmentation.
Helped management make data-driven business decisions.
